Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025
Effective Date: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Fact-It ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

By using Fact-It, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

License Key / Subscription Information:

  • License key (when you activate a paid subscription)
  • Email address (only if you subscribe to paid tier)
  • Subscription tier (Free, Pro, Business)

We DO NOT collect:

  • ❌ Your name (unless voluntarily provided for support)
  • ❌ Payment information (handled by Stripe)
  • ❌ Passwords
  • ❌ Social media credentials

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

Usage Data:

  • Number of fact-checks performed per day
  • Timestamp of fact-check requests
  • Platform used (Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook)
  • Extension version
  • Browser type and version

Technical Data:

  • Browser user agent
  • Operating system
  • Extension errors (for debugging)

We DO NOT collect:

  • ❌ Your browsing history
  • ❌ Content of your posts (unless you explicitly click "Check Claim")
  • ❌ Your personal social media data
  • ❌ Your contacts or followers
  • ❌ Your location (no GPS or IP-based tracking)

2.3 Text Content Sent for Fact-Checking

When you click "Check Claim":

  • The text of the post is sent to our backend API
  • Our API forwards it to AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Perplexity, Groq)
  • The text is processed to detect and verify factual claims
  • Text is NOT stored permanently (only cached for 24 hours to prevent duplicate requests)
  • AI providers may retain data according to their policies (see Section 6)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Provide the Service

  • Process fact-checking requests
  • Validate your subscription tier
  • Enforce usage limits (100 checks/day for free tier)
  • Deliver results to you

3.2 Improve the Service

  • Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Develop new features based on usage data

3.3 Customer Support

  • Respond to your support requests
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Send important service updates (e.g., subscription expiration)

3.4 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect against fraud and abuse

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

AI Providers (for fact-checking):

  • OpenAI (GPT-4o) - Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude 3.5) - Privacy Policy
  • Perplexity (Sonar Pro) - Privacy Policy
  • Groq (Llama 3.1) - Privacy Policy

Payment Processor:

  • Stripe (handles all payment processing)
  • We NEVER see your credit card information
  • Stripe Privacy Policy

4.2 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We will NEVER:

  • ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
  • ❌ Share your data with advertisers
  • ❌ Use your data for marketing to third parties

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law:

  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Government investigations
  • To protect our legal rights
  • To prevent harm or illegal activity

5. Data Retention

How long we keep your data:

Data Type Retention Period
Usage logs 90 days
Subscription data Duration of subscription + 1 year
Cached fact-checks 24 hours
Support emails 2 years after resolution
License keys Until account deletion

After retention period: Data is permanently deleted from our systems.

6. Third-Party AI Provider Policies

When you use Fact-It, text content is processed by AI providers. Each has their own privacy policy:

  • OpenAI: Data retention 30 days (API usage), Location: USA
  • Anthropic: Not stored permanently, Location: USA
  • Perplexity: Per their policy, Location: USA
  • Groq: Not stored (inference only), Location: USA

By using Fact-It, you acknowledge that your fact-check requests are processed by these third parties.
